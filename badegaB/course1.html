Navodaya Vidyalaya

Navodaya School establishment was started in 1986. Navodaya Vidyalayas are run by the Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti (NVS), an autonomous organisation under the Ministry of Education, Department of School Education & Literacy, Govt. of India.

There are total 661 Navodaya School in our country.
