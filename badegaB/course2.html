Rashtriya Indian Military College

Rashtriya Indian Military College (RIMC) is a school for boys and girls situated in doon valley, dehradun. It was established in 1901 and it's name was the Prince Of Wales Royal Indian Military College at that time.

Note

  1. One exam is conducted in July and the other in January.
  2. Admission for boys and girls candidates in the 8th class.

The candidate should be in age bracket of 11(1/2) to 13 years. As on 1 Jan 2024, i.e, they shouldn't be born earlier than 02 Jan 2011 and not later than 01 July 2012. Born not earlier than 02 july 2007 and not later than 01 July 2008.

Exam Pattern

Subject No. Of Questions Marks
English 19 125
Maths 30 200
General Knowledge 75 75
TOTAL 80 100

The candidate should be studying in class 7th or passed class 7th from any recognised school at the time of admission in RIMC, i.e, on 1 Jan 2024.
