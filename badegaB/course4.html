Rashtriya Military School

Rashtriya Military School (RMS) was established in 1946. The schools are of category "A" and administrated by directorate general of military training at IHQ of MOD(ARMY).

There are total 5 RMS school across the country

  1. RMS Chail, Himachal Pradesh
  2. RMS Ajmer, Rajasthan
  3. RMS Dholpur, Rajasthan
  4. RMS Belgaum, Karnataka
  5. RMS Bangalore, Karnataka
